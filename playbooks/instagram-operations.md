# Instagram Operations Playbook

## Core Stance

Reels are the main organic growth engine. Carousels build saves and authority. Stories build relationship. Static posts support identity and trust.

Instagram should be treated as both a recommendation engine and a search engine.

## Priority Stack

1. Niche clarity / topic cluster.
2. Originality.
3. Retention.
4. Saves and shares.
5. Relationship strength.
6. Search discoverability.
7. Consistent cadence.

## Format Strategy

### Reels

Use for discovery and non-follower reach.

Targets:

- 15–30 seconds for fast viral ideas.
- 60–90 seconds for story, education, or breakdowns.
- Strong first 3 seconds.
- Clear payoff or loop.
- Captions/subtitles always.

Good hook types:

- mistake,
- result,
- secret,
- story,
- list,
- tutorial,
- comparison,
- contrarian take,
- pain callout.

### Carousels

Use for saves, frameworks, checklists, myths, tutorials, and authority.

Rules:

- 5–10 slides.
- Slide 1 is the hook.
- Every slide must create value and continuation.
- Last slide has CTA.

### Stories

Use for relationship, trust, and audience feedback.

Rules:

- Post daily when possible.
- Use polls, questions, quizzes, sliders.
- Share new Reels/posts to Stories.
- Use behind-the-scenes and opinion prompts.

## Instagram SEO

Keywords matter more than old hashtag stuffing.

Use keywords in:

- name field,
- bio,
- captions,
- alt text,
- hashtags,
- spoken words/subtitles when possible.

Hashtags:

- 5–15 relevant/specific tags.
- Avoid generic broad tags.

## Caption Rules

- First 125 characters carry the hook.
- Natural keyword use.
- Conversational tone.
- Specific CTA.
- Separate hashtags if needed.

## 30-Minute Engagement Window

For important posts:

1. Engage in niche for 15 minutes before posting.
2. Publish.
3. Reply to every comment within first 30 minutes.
4. Engage in niche for 15 minutes after posting.
5. Share to Stories immediately.

## Weekly Minimum Cadence

- 3 Reels.
- 2 Carousels.
- 5 Stories.

Sweet spot when sustainable:

- 4–5 Reels/week.
- 2–3 Carousels/week.
- Stories daily.

## Metrics That Matter

Track weekly:

- Reel completion rate.
- Saves per 1,000 views.
- Shares per 1,000 views.
- Profile visits from non-followers.
- Comment quality.
- Follows from content.
- Leads, replies, clicks, or offer interest.

## GrowthForge Rules

- Do not chase virality that damages positioning.
- Tie content to operational intelligence, AI-native business systems, automation, monetization, execution, or audience transformation.
- Build reusable assets: hook bank, template bank, keyword bank, experiment log.
