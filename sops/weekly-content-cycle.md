# Weekly Content Cycle SOP

## Objective

Turn research into consistent Instagram execution for GrowthForge.

## Weekly Rhythm

### Day 1 — Research + Topic Selection

- Review previous week metrics.
- Collect cross-platform signals.
- Pick 3–5 priority topics.
- Update hook bank and keyword bank.

### Day 2 — Content System + MUSE Copy Briefing

Create:

- content pillar map or pillar updates,
- scored idea backlog,
- 3–5 Reel briefs,
- 2–3 carousel briefs,
- 5–10 Story prompts,
- MUSE hook variants,
- captions and TTA/CTAs,
- media direction notes,
- experiment cards.

### Day 3–4 — Production

- Record/edit Reels.
- Design carousels.
- Prepare Story sequences.
- QA hooks, captions, and CTAs.

### Day 5–7 — Publishing + Engagement

- Publish according to schedule.
- Use 30-minute engagement window for important posts.
- Reply to comments and DMs.
- Capture early signals.

## Weekly Review

Track:

- best hook,
- best format,
- saves/share rate,
- completion rate,
- profile visits,
- follower quality,
- replies/leads,
- lesson learned,
- next experiment.

## Output

End each week with:

- content shipped,
- metrics summary,
- winning pattern,
- losing pattern,
- next week content bets.
